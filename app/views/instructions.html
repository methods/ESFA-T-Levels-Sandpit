T-Levels instructions

The T Level data collected from the October 2020 return will be used to inform your final T Level allocations, Early Adopter Development funding and Specialist Equipment Allocations. The figures provided in your return should reflect your total planned student numbers for the academic year being completed.

  • If a return is not provided, or a figure is not entered against a T Level you are signed up to deliver this will impact on your allocations and EADF payments.
  • If you enter a zero we will take this as a request to drop the T Level and we will confirm this change with you.
  • Any annexes returned with a yellow incomplete box will be returned for completion so that we can ensure your allocations reflect your planned delivery.

Providers are only required to complete and return by 30 October 2020 the cells highlighted in yellow which have been locked to correspond with the T Levels you have signed up to deliver and should include:

  1. T Level planned student numbers (Year 1) for the 2021/22 AY for each of your approved T Level pathways (column G) - informs T Level allocations and EADF payments.
  2. T Level planned student numbers (Y1 and Y2) for the 2022/23 AY for each of your approved T Level pathways (column I).
  3. T Level planned student numbers (Y1 and Y2) for the 2023/24 AY for each of your approved T Level pathways (column K).
  4. T Level indicative student numbers for the 2024/25 AY (Y1 and Y2) for each of your approved T Level pathways (column L) - informs Specialist Equipment Allocations.

If you wish to extend your T Level delivery in the 2022/23 AY, you can do this by completing the T Level Extension Request tab within this form. This will be subject to approval.

The data provided in the October 2020 return is a reporting requirement in return for providers Early Adopter Development Funding.

Transition Programme Instructions

The Transition Programme data collected from the October 2020 return will be used to inform your Early Adopter Development funding and provider engagement in the delivery of Transition Programme. The figures provided in your return should reflect your total planned student numbers for the academic year being completed.

  • If a return is not provided, or a figure is not entered against a Transition Programme you are signed up to deliver this will impact on your EADF payments.
  • If you enter a zero we will assume you are NOT delivering this Transition Programme.
  • Any annexes returned with a yellow incomplete box will be returned for completion.

Providers are only required to complete and return by 30 October 2020 the cells highlighted in yellow which have been locked to correspond with the T Levels you have signed up to deliver and should include:

  1. Transition programme planned student numbers (Year 1) for the 2021/22 AY aligned to the T Level pathways you are approved to deliver (column G) - informs EADF payments.
  2. Transition programme planned student numbers (Y1 and Y2) for the 2022/23 AY aligned to the T Level pathways you are approved to deliver (column I) - informs provider engagement.

If you wish to extend your T Level delivery in the 2022/23 AY, you can do this by completing the T Level Extension Request tab within this form. This will be subject to approval.

The data provided in the October 2020 return is a reporting requirement in return for providers Early Adopter Development Funding.

Specialisms 21-22 Delivery

The Specialisms data collected from the October 2020 return could be used to inform your final T Level allocations. The figures provided in your return should reflect your total planned students numbers for the 2021/22 academic year and align with the figures entered for T Levels.

  • If a return is not provided, or a figure is not entered against a T Level you are signed up to deliver this may impact on your allocations.
  • If you enter a zero, or field is left blank we will assume you are NOT delivering this Specialism.

Providers are only required to complete and return by 30 October 2020, the cells highlighted in yellow for the specialims you plan to offer and which have been locked to correspond with the T Levels you have signed up to deliver and should include:

  1. Specialisms planned student numbers (Year 1) for the 2021/22 AY aligned to the T Level pathways you are approved to deliver (column G) - may inform T Level allocations.
  2. PAUSED Specialisms - If you are interested in delivering any of the paused Specialisms, please highlight this by inserting a 'Yes' in the corresponding specialism (column G) using the dropdown provided. We would urge you to plan for the paused specialisms not to go ahead and will contact you again should they restart for the 2021/22 academic year.

If you wish to extend your T Level delivery in the 2022/23 AY, you can do this by completing the T Level Extension Request tab within this form. This will be subject to approval.

The data provided in the October 2020 return is a reporting requirement in return for providers Early Adopter Development Funding.

Specialisms 22-23 Interest

Providers are only required to complete and return by 30 October 2020, the cells highlighted in yellow (column F) as an indication of the specialisms you may deliver from 2022 onwards along with an indicative learner number (Year 1 and Year 2) figure (column G). These specialisms do not need to align with the T Levels tab, as are an indication of what you may want to introduce in future years. We will collect planned figures for these specialisms in May 2021.

T-Level Extension Request 22-23

Only to be completed if a provider wants to extend their T Level delivery beyond the T Levels they are signed up to deliver in the 2022/23 Academic Year which you confirmed as part of the May 2020 statistical annex return.

If the T Level is within a route that the provider is not currently approved to deliver (as published at Gov.uk), the request will be reviewed against the T Level eligibility criteria and the providers readiness to deliver the additional T Level(s)

Column I will give you an indication of eligibility against the learner number criteria, but is not a final decision

If the request is supported, the delivery of the T Level will be subject to the provider successfully completing the Awarding Body registration and assessment.

You are also required to enter 'yes' against the pathway you wish to deliver (column F) your planned learner (Year 1) figures (column G) for the additional T Levels and corresponding Transition Programme (column H) if applicable.

The information provided as part of this tab will be used to inform future T Level Allocations.

If you have a question in relation to completing this form please submit this to Statistical.ANNEXES@education.gov.uk and detail "Oct Stat Annex Return" in the subject header
